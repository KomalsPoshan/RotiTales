Roti Tales is a social media initiative by **Poshan, Inc.** ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website.

## Information We Collect

**Analytics Data** — We use Statsig to collect anonymous usage data such as pages visited, session duration, and device type. This helps us understand how visitors interact with our site and improve the experience.

**No Personal Data** — We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information through this website. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking across other websites.

## Third-Party Services

Our site embeds YouTube videos. When you view these videos, YouTube may collect data according to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Social media links on our site direct you to third-party platforms (Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn), each governed by their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
